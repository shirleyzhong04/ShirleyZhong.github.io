Exercise 5

This exercise contains a variety of plants and arrays! My groupmates and I each made two arrays - one being a numerical grid array while the other was a text array. I'll be showing both of mine, Vince's numerical array, and Courtney's text array. There is a total of 10 mapped images. One is on page 0, two are on page 1, five on page 2, and two on page 3. I made 8 functions total in order to show these plants off:

mapToMonoPixels: changes the numerical grid arrays to small black and white squares.

mapToColorPixels: displays numerical grid arrays in squares.

mapToColorShapes: displays hyacinth in its original form.

mapToColorText: displays cherry blossom in its original form.

mapToBitMaps: changes text arrays to images.

mapToTintedBitMaps: changes text arrays to images, second version.

mapToColorText2: translates Courtney's cactus.

mapToColorShapes2: changes Vince's rose.

Project 3

I've never thought about visualizing and mapping out my life besides a simple written out schedule or to-do list. With this project, it was interesting to create a 24-hour schedule of my life into something I could just sit back and watch. I had a hard time in terms of planning out all my if-statements to make sure that I covered every hour of every day. I also had a hard time trying to make certain events overlap. Additionally, it was all very weird to work with since the code ran in military time, which I am not accustomed to. However, this project was definitely something new and I appreciate being able to experience my life in another way.
